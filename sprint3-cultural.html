Sprint 3 - Cultural

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence (EQ) is a skill that is learned, it is the control and focus of ones thoughts and emotions. This is a skill that we can train and improve. many people consider EQ to be more important that IQ.

There are 3 practices we have focused on for training our EQ.

    1. Attention Training
    2. Conscious Listening
    3. Journaling

Attention training

Attention training is essentially paying attention in the moment, also known as mindfulness. Withing this there are two more aspects, Attention and Meta Attention.

Attention = focusing on something clearly, eg. beat of your heart.

Meta attention = is the phenomenom of thinking about thinking. The "double look" at what our attention is on. Eg. thinking about an emotion we are experiencing

Conscious Listening

Journaling

Journaling will be introducted later in the course, its much like our reflections however private. We will do whats called brain dumping typing out whatever comes across our minds in that moment.
